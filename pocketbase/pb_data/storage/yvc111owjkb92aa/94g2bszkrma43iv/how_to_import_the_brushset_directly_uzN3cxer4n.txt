How to import the Brushset directly into Procreate?



1. Install the CozyBrushery app from the AppStore

2. Open the app, enter your email adress and activate your account

3. On your ipad/iphone where you installed the app, press HERE

4. Your brushset is now automatically activated in the app and you can import it directly to Procreate.
